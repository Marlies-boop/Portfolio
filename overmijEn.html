About me

Hello, my name is Marlies.
I am currently in my second year of Software Game Development at Noorderpoort in Groningen. I have created 6 projects and am working on a homemade VR game at the moment. I also participated in the 2023 and 2024 Global Game Jam in Groningen together with some classmates.

The program I use most now is Unity with C#. I have already made a small start on Unreal Engine 5 with blueprints and Godot with Gd script. Furthermore, I already have a base with making models in Blender. When I finish this course I want to start making games made with passion. My goal is to find a company where I can work on games with an enthusiastic group. I also plan to make some small games myself and put them online to see how I can improve myself.

I live with my parents, little brother, cat and dog. Since I was a kid I have always been gaming with my family. The first console I played on was the Playstation 2. Then I went from Nintendo DS to the WII and the Playstation 4. A year ago I bought and put together a PC myself. I got a Havo degree at Harens Lyceum and then ended up at Noorderpoort where I am now studying.
